Privacy policy

Folio · Last updated July 2026

Short version: your financial data never touches our servers at rest. Files are parsed in memory during a single request and immediately discarded. We store nothing about you or your portfolio.

What Folio does

Folio is a portfolio analyzer for Indian investors. You upload your Consolidated Account Statement (CAS) PDF and transaction history files; Folio parses them, computes valuations, XIRR, capital gains, and tax harvesting suggestions, and returns the results to your browser. Your browser stores the results in its own localStorage. The server is stateless.

Data we receive

When you upload a file, its contents are transmitted to our server over HTTPS, processed in memory to extract the information you requested, and the response is returned to your browser. The file and all data derived from it are discarded at the end of the request — typically within a few seconds. No database write occurs. No logs contain your file contents, PAN number, portfolio holdings, or any personally identifiable financial information.

Data we do not collect

  • We do not store your CAS PDF, transaction history files, or any derivatives of them.
  • We do not store your PAN, name, address, folio numbers, or holding details.
  • We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics.
  • We do not share any data with third parties, because we do not have any data to share.

Price data

When you click "Refresh prices", our server fetches current NAVs from AMFI and stock prices from Yahoo Finance. These requests contain no personal data — only fund scheme codes and stock tickers. Both are public data sources.

Browser storage

After parsing, your portfolio data is stored in your browser's localStorage under the key cas-portfolio-v1. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly upload new files. You can delete it at any time using the "Clear data" button in the app, or by clearing your browser's site data for this domain.

Server logs

Our web server (nginx) logs standard HTTP access information: timestamp, IP address, HTTP method, URL path, and response status code. These logs do not contain request bodies and therefore cannot contain your financial data. Logs are retained for up to 7 days for debugging purposes.

Security

All traffic is encrypted using HTTPS (TLS 1.2+). Upload endpoints are rate-limited to prevent abuse. The server runs no database and holds no persistent state.

Applicable law

Folio is operated from India and is subject to the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023. Given that no personal data is retained, we believe Folio's data handling is inherently compliant with both frameworks. If you have a concern, contact us at the address below.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, email us at privacy@yourfolio.in (update this address before launching).
